Answer Few Simple Questions

Just answer some simple questions about your home and existing heating system such as the current boiler type, number of bedrooms and showers & a few more.

This helps our intelligent system to figure out which type & what power boilers will be best suited for your home.

Once you’ve completed the online process, No site survey is needed. You’re all set for a hassle-free

Boiler Replacement.

Choose A Package

You’ll see a range of boilers recommended by us perfectly suited to your heating and hot water needs, Choose one by comparing brands and prices that suit your budget.

No extra or hidden costs. Everything is included within your boiler replacement cost. Removing the pushy salespeople from the process helps us to give you competitive & fixed prices.

Pick An Install Date

Once you’ve made your choice, select an installation date from the calendar that suits you best. Order before 4 pm & you can even choose the next day (subject to availability).

If you can’t find a suitable day on the calendar call our office on 0300 303 0902 to check if we can arrange.

Confirm Your Order

After selecting your boiler install date, choose a payment method you’d like to use to pay on completion. Then fill the required fields of our booking form with your name, email, phone number, boiler installation address & click Confirm Order button.

If you need any assistance ring us on 0300 303 0902

We Box Off Your Installation

Once you’ve confirmed your order our team will call you to double check on what you’ve ordered ensuring nothing is missed and will confirmed the name of engineer coming to fit your new boiler. We don’t use sub-contractors they are our own fitters.

Talk to our team if you require any additional material or services which are not a part of your quote.

On The Day

Our engineers arrive with all ordered materials so there is no time wasted waiting for unnecessary deliveries.

Our Chap’s are friendly, familiar, right on time, and will walk you through what the job involves and how they’ll go about it. So you can relax and leave it to them.

Once the job is done and dusted, they’ll take away your old product as well as any debris from the site.

You Pay & Receive

Once the job is complete & your new boiler is tested to current standards, our engineer will explain how to use your new boiler & controls.

Then you pay us using the method of payment you had selected during the ordering process.

Once payment is done, before leaving our engineer with issue & handover gas safe certificate along with boiler book and other installation documents signed by him.

Quality Checks & We Follow up

Whilst you enjoy the comforts of your new boiler our team members audits the engineer’s full report with images submitted for quality checks. We do it to make sure our good quality promise is delivered.

Your feedback is as important as getting business from you. You’ll receive a call after a few days to check and make sure you stay happy & warm.
