From Where

It All Began

DETERMINED PAST

A family ran business founded By Mr. A Tariq started its journey in 2009. A visionary who worked tirelessly with his team to reach out to customers with better options. Under his guidance company bagged many compliance certifications of FCA, Gas-safe, Sustainable Energy Technologies, MCS, PAS2030, Green deal, and Energy Company Obligation (ECO) assessors & providers. Things got serious by 2012 as the company stepped into joint ventures with major green deal providers, working along with Energy Giants EDF and EON installing A-Rated condensing boilers to reduce carbon footprints under the Govt funded ECO scheme. With a consistent growth, the company achieved another landmark by shaking hands with Worcester Bosch Thermotechnologies Uks leading boiler manufacturer, and kept on working with the representatives & team of engineers successfully satisfying customers, landlords, and housing societies both in the domestic and commercial sectors providing top-notch heating solutions.

PROMISING FUTURE

In 2017, A young & experienced engineer Mr. Shahab Gulfam took over as Managing Director. He was concerned and a bit uncomfortable with old fashioned ways of this industry, according to him customers were being overcharged due to the overheads of salesmen and extra staff to chase them, and started looking for a better customer-focused solution. Having Decided that he teamed up with Mr. S Murtaza ( Engineering Designs ) & Mr. Kamran ( IT ) & used his field experience to successfully design & develop an online intelligent system. He didnt want to play with a few cards short in a deck and the system was put live for testing in Jan 2018. He knew it will take a while to bring perfection and with serious hard work & team effort we now in 2021 Proudly Say We Have Nailed It. This is not an end its the beginning of a new era of heating. Our team is committed to putting all their efforts and experience into developing better solutions in the coming future.
